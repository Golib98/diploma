What is Research Assistant?

Research assistant search system involves a reciprocal relationship between research supervisors and research assistants. Due to the development of a system to help professors find assistant for scientific research, teaching assistantship or graduate assistantship.

Organization All organization content, aggregated and organized in a matter of weeks, not months or years.

Professor Better student-advisor match helps to optimize faculty investment into the right student.

Assistant More time spent on research, less on searching for resources.
